The One Day Industry visit series, or the ODIs, as we call it, is a unique offering by Purple Squirrel to make industry visits more accessible to the student. A student can sign up for ODIs of his/her choice to get a closer look at how those companies operate.

We have an interesting selection of ODIs happening in Delhi and they span across industries

How PR can build brands?

19th June

Speaking of brand value, no one understands its importance more than Avian Media, a leading PR firm that has worked with Sony, BMW and Jabong. Visit their office to get an inside look into the world of PR.

How to become the world's largest selling biscuit?

19th June

Watch and learn how a huge brand functions. A visit to the Parle plant will teach you all the basics of raw material storage, automation systems, quality control and brand value.

What makes Yakult a household name?

22nd June

Before Yakult's foray into the market, probiotic drinks were never a grocery list staple. Visit their state-of-the-art facility to get a better understanding of distribution channels and the supply chain.



Control a Robot with your phone

20th June

Did you know your smartphone could control robots? That's precisely what you'll learn to do at this super-interesting workshop by EHT.
